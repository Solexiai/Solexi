# Solexi

**When you die, who can access your digital life?**

Solexi is a human infrastructure designed to help people organize and transmit
what matters  photos, passwords, documents, accounts, stories, and intentions 
so their loved ones aren’t left with chaos.

## Status
Early-stage project.  
Public frontend and content.  
Backend systems under private development.

## Philosophy
Solexi focuses on human continuity not simulation or replacement.

## Scope
- Digital life organization
- Memory and intent structuring
- Post-life access clarity for loved ones
