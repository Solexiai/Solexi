Glossary — Digital Life After Death

Digital life after death

Digital life after death refers to the state of a person’s digital accounts, files, and online presence after they have died, and the challenges of access, control, and transmission.

Digital chaos

Digital chaos describes the lack of clarity and structure surrounding digital assets, passwords, and accounts after death.

Intent-based transmission

Intent-based transmission is the explicit decision-making process that defines what digital elements are transmitted, to whom, and why.
