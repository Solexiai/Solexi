The System

Three core mechanisms reduce chaos, preserve intent, and improve decision clarity for future executors and beneficiaries.

Structured Vault

Information is organized with clear structure and governance so heirs are not forced to decode scattered files, accounts, and instructions under pressure.

Controlled Access Architecture

Permissions are role-based and intent-driven. Access is scoped by purpose using least-privilege principles to reduce unnecessary exposure.

Verified Delivery Framework

Release workflows use time triggers, multi-step verification, inactivity detection, and controlled execution paths before delivery events are completed.

Why Structured Access Matters

  • Digital silence creates friction when context is missing.
  • Executors often face complexity across systems, credentials, and responsibilities.
  • Intent without structure creates ambiguity at critical moments.

Solexi is designed as preventive digital infrastructure.

FAQ

Is this digital immortality?

No. Solexi is designed for structured digital continuity and controlled transfer of information.

Who controls access?

You define and update access policy using role-based permissions and intent-driven controls.

What if I change beneficiaries?

You can update beneficiaries and access instructions as your personal or family context changes.

Can I export everything?

Data export and portability are part of the system design objective.

What happens if Solexi evolves?

The platform roadmap is continuity-focused, with conservative infrastructure choices and portable data principles.
