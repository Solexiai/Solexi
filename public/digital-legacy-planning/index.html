Family Planning

Digital legacy planning for families in the U.S.

Digital legacy planning helps families reduce confusion after death or incapacity by organizing access instructions, key documents, and personal memory archives before they are urgently needed.

What to plan first

  • Who should access which accounts, records, and devices.
  • Where to store insurance, legal, and identity records securely.
  • How to preserve personal messages and context for loved ones.

Why this matters for U.S. families

Even with legal authority, relatives still face practical barriers: 2FA, unknown subscriptions, scattered files, and missing instructions. A private family vault reduces administrative burden and prevents avoidable delays.
