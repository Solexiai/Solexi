Research

Digital life after death is a real-world problem.

This page curates public references about digital legacy, account access after death, platform policies, and the administrative burden families face. Solexi is built to address the structural gap — not to speculate about consciousness.

Key themes

  • Platform policies (memorialization, access requests, account closure)
  • Legal process (estate, executor access, consent and verification)
  • Security constraints (passwords, 2FA, device encryption)
  • Family burden (time, uncertainty, missed assets/liabilities)
  • Need for intent (explicit instructions reduce conflict and guesswork)

Solexi’s position

Solexi focuses on intent-based structure and responsible transmission. It is not an autonomous AI persona or improvising digital replica. It does not claim digital immortality or simulated consciousness.
