Secure family records vault for trusted access

A secure family records vault gives families one reliable location for key documents, account references, and inheritance preparation notes. The goal is clarity and controlled access, not public exposure.

What to include

  • Identity and estate documents.
  • Critical account and subscription inventories.
  • Instructions for posthumous messages and memorial archives.
