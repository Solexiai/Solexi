AI Policy

Solexi.ai aims to use AI in a consent-first, safety-first way. This page explains what AI features do, what data they use, and how users keep control. Replace TODO blocks with your real implementation.

Core Principles

  • Consent-first: AI features are opt-in where required.
  • Data minimization: use only what’s necessary for the feature.
  • Transparency: explain what inputs are used and what outputs are generated.
  • User control: exports, deletion rules, and clear access management.
Non-negotiable: Never claim “we don’t store anything” or “we don’t see anything” unless it’s technically true and documented.

What AI Can Do (Examples)

  • Search & organization: tagging people/places, semantic retrieval, summaries linked to originals.
  • Guided storytelling: prompts/questions to help you record meaningful context.
  • Optional conversational layer: Q&A over your content (permissioned).

Safety Controls

  • Access gating: AI outputs respect the same permissions as the underlying vault content.
  • Audit events: log AI requests (feature invoked, time, account) without exposing private content.
  • Abuse prevention: rate limits + anomaly detection for suspicious usage.

User Rights

  • Export: you can export your data in documented formats.
  • Deletion: you can request deletion; explain how backups are handled.
  • Consent changes: turn AI features off; explain what persists.
