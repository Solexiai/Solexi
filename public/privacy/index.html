Compliance With Applicable Laws

1. Privacy & Data Protection Laws

  • Canada (Federal): Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).
  • Québec: Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector (as amended by Law 25).
  • European Union (if applicable): General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
  • United States (as applicable): State-level privacy laws (e.g., CCPA/CPRA).

2. Intellectual Property & USPTO Patent Law

Solexi respects all applicable intellectual property laws and regulations administered by the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO).

  • 35 U.S.C. §101–103: Patent eligibility, novelty, and non-obviousness requirements.
  • 35 U.S.C. §112: Specification and enablement requirements.
  • 35 U.S.C. §271: Patent infringement provisions.
  • USPTO Rules of Practice: 37 CFR Part 1.

If Solexi holds or files patent applications with the USPTO, we ensure compliance with disclosure obligations, inventorship requirements, and confidentiality safeguards related to unpublished patent applications.

Note: Patent law governs intellectual property protection and does not replace data protection law obligations.
