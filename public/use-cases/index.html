Use Cases

Built for real life — and what happens after.

Solexi is designed for people who want to reduce the burden on loved ones. Below are real scenarios where structure and intent matter most.

1) Families

A parent dies. The family needs access to email, devices, cloud storage, subscriptions, insurance files, bank portals, and important documents — but nothing is centralized.

  • What exists?
  • Where is it stored?
  • Who has access — and how?

Solexi provides a structured map so families can act without guessing.

2) End-of-life planning

Someone wants peace of mind. Not “digital immortality” — just clarity. They want to decide what should be transmitted, to whom, and why.

  • Key documents and access points
  • Messages and memories to leave behind
  • Explicit intent to reduce future conflict

Solexi turns a sensitive subject into a responsible structure.

3) Creators & entrepreneurs

A creator or founder has assets spread across platforms: domains, hosting, email, cloud drives, payment tools, social accounts, IP documents, and admin access.

  • Business continuity after death
  • Transfer of access without chaos
  • Preservation of intent, not just files

Solexi helps prevent operational collapse and lost assets.
