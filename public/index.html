What We Do

Solexi.ai is a digital legacy preservation platform founded by Daniel Tanguay. Our mission: reduce the confusion left to loved ones — in case of incapacity, or after death.

No legal jargon. No appointments. A guided space where you organize what matters — memories, documents, access, wishes — at your own pace, in serenity.
