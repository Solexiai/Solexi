# Solexi  Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the high-level direction of Solexi.ai
It focuses on clarity of intent rather than timelines or feature promises.

## Phase 1  Awareness & Understanding
Establish a shared understanding of digital life after death.

1.1 Public content and education
1.2 Clear framing of the problem of digital chaos
1.3 Language and concepts accessible to non-technical audiences

## Phase 2  Structuring Digital Life
Enable individuals to organize what matters.

2.1 Clarification of accounts, documents, memories, and intentions
2.2 Human-first structure, not data accumulation
2.3 Emphasis on responsibility toward loved ones

## Phase 3  Access & Continuity Frameworks
Define how access should work after death.

3.1 Explicit intent-based access rules
3.2 Respect for legal, ethical, and emotional boundaries
3.3 No simulation, no identity replacement

## Phase 4  Trust & Long-Term Continuity
Build Solexi as a trusted long-term infrastructure.

4.1 Stability and durability over novelty
4.2 Alignment with families, institutions, and legal frameworks
4.3 Focus on intergenerational continuity

## Guiding Principle
Solexi prioritizes clarity, restraint,  responsibility
over speed, hype, speculative technology.
